01What is FocusNest?

FocusNest is a distraction-free study tool built for university students. It combines the Pomodoro technique with ambient sounds, progress tracking, and achievement badges — all in one lightweight app that works directly in your browser.

No account needed. No installation. No distractions. Your data stays on your device.

⏱
Pomodoro Timer
Structured focus and break sessions to keep your mind fresh.
🎵
Ambient Sounds
Rain, Café, or Forest — block out distractions with soothing loops.
📊
Study Stats
Track daily and weekly focus time, session counts, and streaks.
🏆
Achievements
Unlock badges as you build consistent study habits.

02Using the Timer

The timer is the heart of FocusNest. It follows the Pomodoro Technique — work in focused bursts, then take short breaks to recharge.

Starting your first session

1
Open the Timer tabIt is selected by default when you open the app.
2
Choose your modeSelect Focus (25 min), Short Break (5 min), or Long Break (15 min) from the tabs at the top.
3
Press StartThe countdown begins and the ring animates as time passes.
4
Work until the chimeA three-note chime plays when your session ends. Take your break!
5
Repeat the cycleAfter every 4 focus sessions, a long break is triggered automatically.

Timer controls

▶
Start / Pause / ResumeThe main blue button. Tap once to start, again to pause, again to resume.
↺
ResetThe left icon button. Resets the current session back to zero — does not count as a completed session.
⏭
SkipThe right icon button. Moves to the next session type (Focus → Break or Break → Focus) without counting a completion.
💡The timer uses your device's system clock, so it stays accurate even when the tab is minimised or your screen is off.

Auto-transition

Enable the Auto-transition sessions toggle to have FocusNest automatically start the next session after a short pause — no tapping needed.

Session cycle

FocusNest tracks your cycle automatically:

1
Focus → Short Break → Focus → Short Break → Focus → Short Break → Focus → Long Break — then the cycle repeats. The four dots below the timer show your progress in the current cycle.

03Ambient Sounds

Background audio helps mask distractions and signal your brain that it is time to focus. FocusNest generates all sounds locally — no streaming, no internet required after the page loads.

1
🌧 RainSteady white noise — ideal for deep concentration and blocking out office or café chatter.
2
☕ CaféLow ambient murmur with soft tones — good for creative work and mild background energy.
3
🌲 ForestGentle natural ambience with slow modulation — calming for reading and writing tasks.

Controls

Tap any sound button to start playback. Tap it again to stop. Only one sound plays at a time. Use the volume slider to adjust independently of your device volume.

⚠️On mobile (iOS Safari, Android Chrome), tap a sound button once after the page loads to start audio. This is a browser security requirement — audio cannot start automatically without a user tap.

Chime mute

Use the Session chime toggle in the audio panel to silence the end-of-session tone without affecting ambient sound. The two controls are fully independent.

04Stats Dashboard

Tap Stats in the navigation to see your study history. Stats update automatically after every completed focus session.

📅
TodayTotal focus minutes and session count for today only.
📆
This WeekCumulative focus minutes and sessions over the last 7 days.
🔥
Day StreakHow many consecutive days you have completed at least one focus session. Miss a day and the streak resets.

Weekly chart

The bar chart shows your focus minutes for each of the last 7 days. Hover over a bar to see the exact minutes.

Recent sessions

A log of your last 8 sessions showing the date, time, and duration of each one.

💡Only fully completed sessions count. If you reset the timer mid-session, it does not appear in your stats. Sessions where you pressed skip also do not count.

06Settings & Configuration

All settings are in the right panel on the Timer page. Changes take effect immediately.

Session lengths

−+
FocusDefault 25 minutes. Adjust from 1–99 minutes. Standard Pomodoro is 25 min.
−+
Short BreakDefault 5 minutes. Take a real break — stand up, stretch, hydrate.
−+
Long BreakDefault 15 minutes. Triggered automatically after every 4 focus sessions.
💡If you change a session length while the timer is stopped, it resets to the new duration. Changing it while running has no effect until the next session.

07Keyboard Shortcuts

FocusNest supports full keyboard navigation. Use Tab to move between controls and Enter or Space to activate them.

SpaceStart / Pause / Resume the timer
SSkip to next session
RReset current session
TabSwitch between Focus → Short Break → Long Break (cycles)
↑Volume up (+5%)
↓Volume down (−5%)
💡Shortcuts work anywhere on the page — no need to click first. They are disabled when typing in an input field.

08Tips & Best Practices

Getting the most from Pomodoro

✓
Pick one task per sessionWrite down what you want to achieve before pressing Start. Single-tasking beats multitasking every time.
✓
Actually take the breakStep away from the screen during breaks. Your brain consolidates information when you rest.
✓
Build the streakEven one 25-minute session per day maintains your streak and compounds into significant study hours.
✓
Use ambient sound consistentlyYour brain associates the sound with focus over time — it becomes a ritual trigger.

Data & privacy

All your data — sessions, streaks, badges — is stored only on your device using browser localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server. If you clear your browser data or switch devices, your progress will reset.

⚠️To preserve your progress, avoid clearing browser site data for focusnest. Using private/incognito mode will also mean data does not persist after closing the window.

09FAQ

The timer lost time when I switched tabs — is it broken?

No. FocusNest uses your device's system clock, not a counting variable. When you return to the tab, the timer corrects itself instantly. It is accurate to within ±2 seconds over a full 25-minute session.

Why is there no sound when I tap Rain/Café/Forest?

On mobile browsers, audio requires a user gesture to start. Make sure you tap the sound button directly (not the page background). If it still does not work, try refreshing the page and tapping again.

My stats disappeared — what happened?

Stats are stored in your browser's localStorage. They are reset if you clear browser data, use a different browser, switch devices, or use private/incognito mode.

Does FocusNest work offline?

Yes — after the page first loads, FocusNest works entirely offline. The timer, audio, stats, and badges all function without an internet connection.

Can I use FocusNest on my phone?

Yes. FocusNest is mobile-first and works on iOS Safari (iOS 16+) and Android Chrome. The layout adapts from 360px mobile up to 1440px desktop.

Why does my streak say 0 even though I studied yesterday?

A streak requires at least one fully completed focus session per day. Sessions that were reset or skipped do not count. Check the Stats page to confirm yesterday has a session logged.
